Build with us.

We partner with companies, founders, and organisations who want to support student entrepreneurship at Stirling. Partnerships help us fund events, bring speakers to campus, and provide meaningful opportunities to members.

How partners can get involved

The goal is to make it easy for a potential sponsors to say “yes”.

Speaking

Founder talk / guest session

Host an evening talk and Q&A about your journey, lessons learned, and advice to students.

Workshop

Skills workshop

Run a practical session on product, marketing, finance, pitching, sales, or engineering.

Mentoring

Mentor office hours

Offer monthly 1:1 office hours to help students move ideas forward with expert feedback.

Sponsorship

Event sponsorship

Support refreshments, venue costs, and materials in exchange for brand visibility and engagement.

Recruitment

Careers & opportunities

Share internships, graduate roles, or insight sessions relevant to ambitious students.

Collaboration

Co-hosted events

Partner with us for hack nights, pitch competitions, or interdisciplinary build sessions.

What partners get

Brand visibility

Logo placement on our website, slides, and event promo material (as applicable).

Student engagement

Meet motivated students interested in building, learning, and creating real impact.

Community impact

Support innovation on campus by helping students develop real-world skills.

We keep it simple

Clear expectations, good communication, and events that feel valuable for everyone.

We care about fit

We prioritise partners whose mission aligns with student development.
